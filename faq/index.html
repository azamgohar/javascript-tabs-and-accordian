Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline project ki complexity pe depend karta hai. Simple website usually 3–5 days mein ready ho jaati hai, jabke advanced ya custom features wali site ko 1–2 weeks ya us se zyada lag sakta hai.
